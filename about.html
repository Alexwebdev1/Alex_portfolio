BIO

I live and work in Ukraine, Kyiv. At my day job, I develop any sort of websites and cross-platform mobile apps.
I also freelance when I can, mostly building custom websites and apps that you can’t get from a template.
My formal art & design training taught me to carefully consider my audience and what experience I intend for them to have through my work. Who is visiting your site? What are they looking for? How do they find it? What analytics are available to base our decisions on?
While my focus is mobile-first responsive websites with HTML, JavaScript,  SASS, PHP, and MySQL (often on top of WordPress or Drupal).
I am always experimenting, always learning, and never bored.
